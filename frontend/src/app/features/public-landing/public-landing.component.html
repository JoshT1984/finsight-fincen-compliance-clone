About Finsight

Finsight is a platform for managing Currency Transaction Reports (CTRs) and Suspicious Activity Reports (SARs). It supports BSA/AML workflows and helps compliance teams and law enforcement coordinate on cases from intake through referral and closure.

Key capabilities include:

  • Case management — Work a queue of cases with notes, status tracking, and referral actions.
  • CTR & SAR workflows — Browse and manage CTR and SAR data with clear linkages to cases.
  • Document handling — Upload and view documents tied to CTRs, SARs, and cases.
  • Audit trail — Full history of changes and actions for compliance and review.
